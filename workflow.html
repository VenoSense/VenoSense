Wells Score Integration

Evidence-based clinical decision support

Automated Clinical Assessment

The Wells Score is a clinically validated tool for assessing DVT probability. VenoSense digitizes and streamlines this process, combining clinical inputs with imaging findings for a comprehensive assessment.

Integrated Wells Criteria:

  • Active cancer treatment
  • Recent paralysis, paresis, or immobilization
  • Recent bed rest or major surgery
  • Localized tenderness along deep veins
  • Entire leg swelling
  • Calf swelling >3cm compared to asymptomatic leg
  • Pitting edema in symptomatic leg
  • Collateral superficial veins
  • Previous DVT history
  • Alternative diagnosis as likely as DVT
