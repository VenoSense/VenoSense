Revolutionizing DVT Detection with AI-Powered Ultrasounds

Welcome to VenoSense

VenoSense is an innovative, automated ultrasound system designed to detect deep vein thrombosis (DVT) early and efficiently using advanced non-invasive ultrasound technology combined with AI-driven image analysis. Our mission is to revolutionize vascular health monitoring, making DVT detection more accessible, affordable, and accurate.

With DVT affecting nearly 900,000 individuals annually in the U.S., early detection is crucial. VenoSense streamlines the diagnostic workflow, reduces reliance on specialized personnel, and aims to lower healthcare costs.

Our Technology

The VenoSense system employs a Doppler-based automated probe mounted on a motorized platform with a secure clamp mechanism. This ensures stable, high-quality imaging without continuous manual intervention.

Captured ultrasound images are processed in real-time by a machine learning algorithm that identifies key veins and analyzes blood flow dynamics. By comparing current data against established baselines, the system rapidly detects deviations that may indicate clot formation.

Key Features:

  • Automated Probe System
  • Real-time Machine Learning Analysis
  • Doppler-based Blood Flow Monitoring
  • Secure Clamp Mechanism

Workflow & Scores

VenoSense integrates seamlessly into the DVT diagnostic workflow, providing an automated and efficient solution for clinicians. The system automates the Wells score assessment, reducing variability and improving accuracy.

Diagnostic Workflow:

  1. Patient Assessment
  2. Automated Ultrasound Scan
  3. Real-time Image Analysis
  4. Automated Wells Score Calculation
  5. Report Generation

Risk Factors

VenoSense helps evaluate key risk factors to identify patients at high risk for DVT. These factors include age, obesity, history of vein injury, prolonged inactivity, and clotting disorders.

Common Risk Factors:

  • Age over 65
  • Obesity
  • History of Vein Injury
  • Prolonged Inactivity
  • Clotting Disorders
